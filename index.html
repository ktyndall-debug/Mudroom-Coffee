Our Story

Welcome to The Mudroom

The Mudroom Coffee Company represents more than just exceptional coffee – we're creating a gathering place where community connections flourish and every cup tells a story.

Born from a passion for bringing specialty coffee culture to rural North Carolina, The Mudroom Coffee Company started as a dream shared by longtime Chinquapin residents Sarah and Mike Thompson. After years of traveling and experiencing coffee cultures worldwide, they returned home with a vision: to create a space where neighbors become friends and every visitor feels like family.

Our name reflects our philosophy – just as a mudroom is the welcoming transition space in a home, our coffee shop serves as the heart of our community. Here, farmers grab their morning fuel before heading to the fields, students find a quiet corner to study, and friends gather to catch up over expertly crafted beverages.

We source our beans directly from small farms, ensuring fair trade practices while delivering exceptional flavor profiles. Our partnership with local bakeries and suppliers means every pastry, sandwich, and ingredient tells a story of regional pride and community support.

Our Offerings

Signature Coffees

From classic espressos to our house-blend specialties, each cup showcases our commitment to excellence. Our skilled baristas create beautiful latte art that's almost too pretty to drink.

  • Mudroom House Blend - Our signature medium roast with notes of chocolate and caramel
  • Carolina Sunrise Latte - Espresso with local honey and cinnamon
  • Cold Brew Float - House cold brew with vanilla bean ice cream
  • Seasonal Pumpkin Spice (Fall) & Peppermint Mocha (Winter)

Fresh Pastries

Start your day with our selection of locally sourced, freshly baked pastries. From flaky croissants to seasonal muffins, we have the perfect complement to your morning coffee.

  • Buttermilk Biscuits with Local Honey Butter - Made fresh daily
  • Seasonal Muffins - Blueberry (summer), Apple Cinnamon (fall), Cranberry Orange (winter)
  • Artisan Croissants - Plain, chocolate, and almond varieties
  • Southern Pecan Danish & Gluten-Free Banana Bread

Light Meals

Our carefully crafted sandwiches and light meals feature fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or an afternoon snack with your favorite beverage.

  • The Farmhand - Local eggs, NC country ham, aged cheddar on a fresh biscuit
  • Pulled Pork Grilled Cheese - Carolina BBQ pork with sharp cheddar
  • Garden Fresh Wrap - Mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, local cheese
  • Daily Soup - Rotating favorites like Chicken & Dumplings, Tomato Basil
